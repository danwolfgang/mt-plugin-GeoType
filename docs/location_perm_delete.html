Permanently Removing Locations from a Blog

Removing a location from a blog also removes all of the instances of the location in any entries. It also permanently removes any additional location data.


Figure 1
The GeoType icon on the left leads to a listing of Locations in this blog.






Figure 2
Select a location to permanently delete in the GeoType
Location listing page.



Permanently Deleting a Location
  1. Click on the 'GeoType' icon in the toolbar on the left. [ Figure 1 ]

  2. Check the location you'd like to delete, and click the grey 'Delete' above the listing. [ Figure 2 ]
