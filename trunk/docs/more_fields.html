GoogleMap Settings: Additional Location Fields

Your maps can hold more information than just some points on a grid! Follow these steps to configure and enter that information.

The GeoType icon on the left leads to a listing of Locations in this blog.

Accessing the Locations List

To modify the additional location data, first you'll need to access the Locations listing page.

Click on the 'GeoType' icon in the toolbar on the left. [ Figure 1 ]

Entering Additional Location Data

From the Locations list [ Figure 2 ], click on the name of a particular location.

The example below [ Figure 3 ] uses the 'Iceland' location as an example. Obviously, depending on the scope of the location, not all of these fields may be used, but some (such as the thumbnail or rating) may be universally relevant.




Fill in any (or all) fields; click 'Make Changes' to save.
